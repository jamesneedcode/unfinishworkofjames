Barbell curls

Is a classic bicep exercise where you hold a barbell with an underhand grip and curl it up towards your chest.

how to do it

dumbbell curls

Similar to barbell curls, but using dumbbells instead. You can vary the angle of your grip (neutral, underhand, or overhand) to target different parts of your biceps.

how to do it

Hammer curls

This variation involves holding the dumbbells with a neutral grip (palms facing each other) and curling them up towards your shoulders. This targets the brachialis muscle, which lies underneath the biceps.

how to do it

Concentration curls

This is an isolation exercise where you sit on a bench with one arm braced against your inner thigh and curl a dumbbell towards your shoulder. This helps to target the peak of the biceps.

how to do it
